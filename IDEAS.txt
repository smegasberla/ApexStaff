=====================================================
            APEXSTAFF PLUGIN SPECIFICATIONS
=====================================================

MAIN COMMAND: /apexstaff (Aliases: /as, /staff)

-----------------------------------------------------
1. STEALTH & INVESTIGATION
-----------------------------------------------------
/as vanish
- Permission: apexstaff.vanish
- Action: Toggles visibility. Hides join/quit messages.

/as inspect <player>
- Permission: apexstaff.inspect
- Action: Opens a GUI showing the player's live inventory,
  armor, health, and hunger.

/as follow <player>
- Permission: apexstaff.follow
- Action: Silently teleports you to the player's location
  every tick. Toggle off by moving or re-typing.

/as mount <player>
- Permission: apexstaff.mount
- Action: Silently "rides" the player as a passenger to
  see exactly where they are looking.

/as xraycheck
- Permission: apexstaff.xray
- Action: Lists players with suspicious Diamond-to-Stone
  mining ratios.

-----------------------------------------------------
2. DISCIPLINE & CONTROL
-----------------------------------------------------
/as freeze <player>
- Permission: apexstaff.freeze
- Action: Locks player in place. Sends a warning on
  their screen to "Join Discord/Wait for Staff."

/as notes <player> [add/list/remove] [text]
- Permission: apexstaff.notes
- Action: Internal staff database to track suspicious
  behavior across sessions.

/as staffchat <message> (Alias: /sc)
- Permission: apexstaff.staffchat
- Action: Sends a message visible only to users with
  the staffchat permission.

-----------------------------------------------------
3. CHAT & SERVER UTILITY
-----------------------------------------------------
/as clearchat
- Permission: apexstaff.clearchat
- Action: Clears global chat for all players except staff.

/as slowmode <seconds>
- Permission: apexstaff.slowmode
- Action: Limits how frequently players can send messages.

/as togglealt <player>
- Permission: apexstaff.alts
- Action: Scans the database for other accounts logged
  into the same IP address.

-----------------------------------------------------
4. THE "STAFF MODE" (Recommended Feature)
-----------------------------------------------------
/as mode
- Permission: apexstaff.staffmode
- Action: Toggles a full "Staff Environment."

When ON:
- Saves current player inventory to a local cache.
- Clears inventory and gives "Staff Tools":
  * Slot 1: Compass (Teleport through walls)
  * Slot 2: Book (Notes tool)
  * Slot 3: Ice Block (Freeze/Unfreeze player)
  * Slot 4: Stick (Knockback/Investigation)
  * Slot 9: Dye (Toggle Vanish)
- Automatically enables /vanish and /fly.

When OFF:
- Restores original inventory.
- Disables vanish/fly (unless they had them before).
- Teleports them back to their original location.

-----------------------------------------------------
PERMISSION NODE SUMMARY:
- apexstaff.* (All permissions)
- apexstaff.admin (Reset/Reload plugin)
- apexstaff.mod (Access to investigation/moderate tools)
- apexstaff.helper (Access to staffchat/vanish/notes)
